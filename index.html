  • Hawai'i is extremely popular, so book your flight in advance
  • It is recommended that you have an itinerary for your trip, so you don't waste a single second on your vacation!
  • Make sure you study the language before taking a flight to Hawai'i

History

Hawai'i has a incredible history and you can see clearly the mark that economic imperialism has left.
But in spite of this, Hawai'i's culture continues to shine just as vibrantly. Here is a timeline of events in Hawai'i's history:

  • 940-1200: Arrival of Polynesians
  • 1780's: European and US trading ships start docking in Hawai’i
  • 1795: King Kamehameha unifies Hawaiias
  • 1821: Protestant missionaries begin converting Hawaiians to Christianity
  • 1826: James Honeywell establishes C. Brewer & Co. Ltd Trade and service organization
  • 1835: The first sugar plantation is established on Kauai
  • 1842: The first House of Representatives is called to order.
  • 1843: Lord George Paulet siezes Hawaii for England.
  • 1891: Queen Liliuokalani comes to power
  • 1894: The Republic of Hawaii is established
  • 1900: Hawaii becomes a US territory
  • 1953: Hawaii becomes the 50th state
